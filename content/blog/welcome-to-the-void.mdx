## Why Build Alone?

There's something about solitude that clarifies thought. No committees. No consensus. Just you, the problem, and the solution whispers into existence.

When you build alone, you answer to one authority: **the work itself**.

> "The best work happens when you're alone with the problem long enough to hear the solution whisper itself into existence."
